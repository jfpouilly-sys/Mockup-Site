About AZIT

AZIT is a specialized brand dedicated to industrial communication protocol stacks and safety systems. We focus exclusively on delivering production-ready, certifiable protocol implementations for safety-critical and industrial automation applications.

Our protocol stacks are designed with one core principle: complete transparency and control for our customers. We develop everything in-house, without third-party dependencies, giving you full visibility into your safety-critical communication layer.

Our Mission

To provide industrial equipment manufacturers with protocol stacks that enable rapid development without compromising on safety, quality, or certification requirements.

Our Values

  • Transparency: No black boxes, no hidden dependencies
  • Quality: Production-ready code with comprehensive testing
  • Expertise: Deep knowledge of protocols and safety standards
  • Support: Direct access to the engineers who build the stacks
  • Partnership: Long-term relationships with our customers

About ISIT

ISIT (Innovative Solutions for Intelligent Technologies) is a French engineering company with over 30 years of experience in embedded systems, real-time software, and industrial communication protocols.

Founded in the 1990s, ISIT has built a reputation for delivering high-quality, production-ready embedded solutions to leading industrial manufacturers across Europe.

ISIT Expertise Areas

  • Embedded systems architecture and design
  • Real-time operating systems (RTOS)
  • Industrial communication protocols
  • Safety-critical systems development
  • Hardware abstraction layers (HAL)
  • Device driver development
  • System integration and testing

The AZIT Brand

AZIT was created as a specialized brand within ISIT to focus exclusively on industrial protocol stacks and communication solutions. This specialization allows us to:

  • Maintain deep expertise in specific protocols
  • Provide dedicated support for protocol customers
  • Continuously improve and certify our stacks
  • Build partnerships with certification bodies
  • Develop specialized tools and documentation

Combined Strengths

AZIT customers benefit from both specialized protocol expertise and the broader embedded systems capabilities of ISIT, providing comprehensive support for complete system development.

Our Journey

Key milestones in AZIT's history

1990s - ISIT Founded

ISIT established as an embedded systems engineering company in France, specializing in real-time software and industrial protocols.

2000s - Protocol Stack Development

ISIT begins developing industrial communication protocol stacks for major industrial manufacturers, focusing on CANopen and fieldbus protocols.

2010s - Safety Protocol Focus

Expansion into safety protocols with development of FSoE and CANopen Safety stacks. First Bureau V√©ritas SIL3 certifications achieved.

2015 - AZIT Brand Launch

AZIT brand created to focus exclusively on industrial protocol stacks, providing specialized expertise and dedicated support.

2020 - Portfolio Expansion

Expansion of protocol portfolio to include J1939, OPC UA, and additional industrial Ethernet protocols. ISO 9001 certification achieved.

2025 - Today

AZIT continues to lead in safety-certified protocol stacks, with 15+ protocol implementations and customers across Europe and beyond.
