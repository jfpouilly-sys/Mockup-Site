Frequently Asked Questions

What information should I include in my quote request?

Please provide details about your target hardware platform, RTOS (if any), project timeline, and specific protocol requirements. The more details you provide, the more accurate quote we can prepare.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Our team typically responds to quote requests within 24 hours during business days. For complex projects, we may schedule a follow-up call to discuss your requirements in detail.

Do you offer evaluation licenses?

Yes, we offer evaluation licenses for most of our protocol stacks. Contact us to discuss your evaluation needs and we'll provide you with the necessary documentation and support.

Can you help with safety certification?

Absolutely. Our expertise services include safety compliance consulting, pre-audits, and support throughout your certification process with Bureau V√©ritas or other certification bodies.

What's included in your porting services?

Our porting services include HAL development, BSP integration, testing, validation, and comprehensive documentation. We ensure the stack runs correctly on your target platform.

Do you provide training?

Yes, we offer both on-site and remote training sessions for our protocol stacks and industrial communication standards. Training can be customized to your team's needs.
