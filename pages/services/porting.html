What is Stack Porting?

Porting is the process of adapting a protocol stack to run on your specific hardware platform, MCU, and RTOS environment. It involves implementing the Hardware Abstraction Layer (HAL), integrating with your Board Support Package (BSP), and ensuring the stack operates correctly in your target system.

AZIT Porting Services

With over 30 years of embedded systems expertise, our team specializes in porting industrial protocol stacks to diverse hardware platforms. We handle everything from HAL development to final validation, ensuring your protocol stack integrates seamlessly with your application.

Why Choose Our Porting Services?

While our protocol stacks are designed to be hardware-agnostic and easy to integrate, porting still requires expertise in both the protocol and your target platform. Our porting services accelerate your development, reduce integration risks, and ensure optimal performance.

Our Porting Process

We follow a structured, proven methodology to ensure successful integration of protocol stacks on your target platform.

1. Requirements Analysis

We start by understanding your hardware platform, RTOS, BSP, and specific application requirements. This includes analyzing memory constraints, performance requirements, and integration points.

  • Platform specification review
  • Resource requirements analysis
  • Integration point identification
  • Timeline and milestone definition

2. HAL Development

We implement the Hardware Abstraction Layer that connects the protocol stack to your specific hardware, including timers, interrupts, memory management, and communication interfaces.

  • Timer and interrupt handling
  • Memory allocation routines
  • Hardware interface drivers
  • RTOS integration layer

3. Integration & Testing

We integrate the ported stack with your BSP and application code, then perform comprehensive testing to ensure correct operation under all conditions.

  • BSP integration
  • Functional testing
  • Performance validation
  • Stress and edge case testing

4. Documentation & Delivery

We provide complete documentation of the porting work, including HAL implementation details, integration guide, and knowledge transfer to your team.

  • HAL documentation
  • Integration guide
  • Test reports
  • Knowledge transfer session
