Why Maintenance Support Matters

Industrial systems have long lifecycles, often spanning 10-20 years or more. During this time, protocol standards evolve, new hardware platforms emerge, and bugs may be discovered. Our maintenance support ensures your protocol stack implementation remains reliable, compliant, and up-to-date throughout your product's lifecycle.

AZIT Maintenance Services

We provide comprehensive maintenance and support services for all our protocol stacks, including bug fixes, updates, technical support, and compliance maintenance. Our support plans are designed to give you peace of mind and ensure your industrial systems continue to operate reliably.

Expert Support Team

Our support team includes the same engineers who developed the protocol stacks, ensuring you receive expert assistance from people who know the code inside and out. We provide direct access to our technical experts without layers of intermediaries.

How It Works

1. Issue Reporting

Report issues through our support portal, email, or phone (depending on your plan). Our system automatically creates a ticket and notifies the appropriate engineers.

2. Assessment & Priority

We assess the issue and assign priority level (Critical, High, Medium, Low) based on impact and severity. Critical issues receive immediate attention.

3. Resolution & Delivery

Our engineers work on resolving the issue, keeping you informed of progress. Once fixed, we deliver the patch or update with detailed release notes.

4. Verification & Closure

You verify the fix in your environment. We ensure the issue is fully resolved before closing the ticket and updating our knowledge base.

Priority Levels

Critical

System down or safety function compromised. Immediate response required.

High

Major functionality impaired or performance severely degraded.

Medium

Moderate impact on functionality or performance. Workaround available.

Low

Minor issue or enhancement request with minimal impact.
