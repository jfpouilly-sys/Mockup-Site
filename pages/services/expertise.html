Our Expertise

With over 30 years of experience in embedded systems and industrial protocols, AZIT provides comprehensive expertise to support your most demanding projects.

Safety Compliance

AZIT provides comprehensive support for safety certification and compliance with IEC 61508, ISO 13849, and IEC 61784-3 standards. Our experts guide you through the entire certification process, from pre-audits and gap analysis to Bureau Veritas coordination.

Protocol Development

AZIT delivers custom protocol implementation and development services tailored to your unique requirements. From custom protocols and extensions to proprietary safety protocols and gateway development, our team builds solutions that go beyond standard protocol stacks.

Testing & Validation

AZIT offers comprehensive testing, conformance validation, and interoperability testing for protocol implementations. Using industry-standard tools and our extensive test infrastructure, we ensure your products meet all requirements before market release.

Learn more about Testing and Validation services

Industrial Networks

AZIT provides tailored solutions for your needs based on field communication protocols. From network training and diagnostics to protocol gateway development and architecture consulting, we support your industrial network projects across CANopen, EtherCAT, FSoE, and more.
