Wireless Safety Communication

Wireless safety systems enable safety-critical communication without physical cables, essential for mobile machinery, cranes, AGVs, and distributed safety applications. Our wireless safety solution will provide reliable, certified safety communication over wireless channels while maintaining the integrity required for SIL2/PLd applications.

AZIT Wireless Safety Stack (In Development)

Our wireless safety implementation will leverage advanced safety protocol techniques including redundant transmission, cryptographic protection, and robust error detection to achieve safety integrity over unreliable wireless media. Compatible with standard wireless technologies including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and industrial wireless protocols.

Key Applications (Planned)

  • Mobile machinery and vehicles
  • Overhead cranes and hoists
  • Automated guided vehicles (AGVs)
  • Warehouse automation
  • Remote emergency stops
  • Distributed safety systems
