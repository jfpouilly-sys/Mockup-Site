What is CIP Safety?

CIP Safety is the safety extension to the Common Industrial Protocol (CIP), providing SIL3/PLe safety communication over standard industrial networks including EtherNet/IP, DeviceNet, and ControlNet. It's widely adopted in North American industrial automation for machine safety applications.

AZIT CIP Safety Stack (In Development)

Our CIP Safety implementation will provide a complete, certifiable solution for safety devices on EtherNet/IP and DeviceNet networks. Built on proven safety architecture principles from our FSoE and CANopen Safety products, it will deliver the reliability and performance required for safety-critical applications.

Key Applications (Planned)

  • Safety PLCs and controllers
  • Safety I/O modules
  • Emergency stop devices
  • Light curtains and scanners
  • Safety drives
  • Machine guarding systems
