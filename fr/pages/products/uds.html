What is UDS?

Unified Diagnostic Services (UDS) is the international standard (ISO 14229) for automotive ECU diagnostics. It defines a comprehensive set of diagnostic services for reading fault codes, programming ECUs, accessing measurement data, and performing routine tests. UDS is mandatory for modern automotive systems and is used extensively in vehicle development, production, and service.
